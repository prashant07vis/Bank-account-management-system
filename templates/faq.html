Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common queries

How do I create a bank account?
From the main menu, click "Create Account" and complete the registration form with your personal details. Once submitted, your account will be created instantly.
How can I deposit money into my account?
Log in to your account → go to the Dashboard → select "Deposit" → enter the amount and confirm with your MPIN.
How do I apply for a loan?
After logging in, navigate to "Apply Loan" from the dashboard, fill in the required details (loan type, amount, purpose), and submit. The status can be tracked under "Loan Status".
What is MPIN and how is it used?
MPIN is your secure 6-digit Mobile Personal Identification Number. It is required to authenticate transactions such as withdrawals, transfers, deposits, and loan applications.
What should I do if my account gets locked?
Account locking usually occurs after multiple incorrect MPIN attempts. Contact the bank administrator immediately (via the admin dashboard or support) to unlock it.
