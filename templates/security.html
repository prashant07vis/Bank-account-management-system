Security Guidelines

Protecting your account is our top priority — and yours too

  • Never share your MPIN with anyone — not even bank staff or family members.
  • Always log out completely after finishing your banking session, especially on shared devices.
  • Avoid public computers or Wi-Fi for accessing your Bharat Bank account.
  • Verify the website URL starts with https:// and shows the official Bharat Bank domain before entering credentials.
  • Never share OTPs, transaction codes, or any verification messages with anyone.
  • Report suspicious activity or unauthorized transactions immediately to the bank administrator.
  • Change your MPIN regularly — especially if you suspect any compromise.
  • Use strong, unique passwords and always ensure you're on a secure, private internet connection.
